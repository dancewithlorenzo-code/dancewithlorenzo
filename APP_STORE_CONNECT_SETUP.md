| **Primary Language** | Japanese |

### 10. Build Upload

After completing all the information above:

1. **Build your app** using EAS Build or Xcode:
   ```bash
   eas build --platform ios
   ```

2. **Upload to App Store Connect**:
   - EAS will automatically upload if configured
   - Or manually upload via Xcode → Window → Organizer

3. **Select the build** in App Store Connect:
   - Go to your app version
   - Under "Build" section, click "+"
   - Select the uploaded build

4. **Submit for Review**:
   - Review all sections for completeness
   - Click "Submit for Review"
   - Apple typically reviews within 24-48 hours

## After Submission

### Expected Timeline
- **Under Review**: 1-2 days
- **In Review**: 1-3 days
- **Pending Developer Release**: You control release timing

### Common Rejection Reasons (Avoid These!)
- ❌ Missing demo account credentials
- ❌ Broken links to privacy policy
- ❌ In-app purchases not functioning
- ❌ App crashes during review
- ❌ Missing required screenshots
- ❌ Payment testing instructions unclear

### What to Do If Rejected
1. Read Apple's feedback carefully
2. Fix the specific issues mentioned
3. Test thoroughly
4. Resubmit with clarification notes
